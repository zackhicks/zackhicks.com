Executive Biography

Founder-operator. Transformation leader. Board director.

Global executive and board director focused on helping Fortune 250 companies translate technology disruption into sustained shareholder value. Trusted advisor to boards and CEOs at the intersection of enterprise AI, cyber resilience and capital allocation.

Independent Director at Signet Jewelers and Chair of the Technology Committee, with service on the Nominating and Governance Committee and Audit subcommittees. As Founder and CEO of Toyota Connected, built and scaled a global mobility platform business. Most recently served as Chief Digital and Technology Officer at Kimberly-Clark, leading enterprise-wide transformation across commercial, supply chain and finance.

Known for translating complex technology investments into clear financial and strategic outcomes. Inducted into the CIO Hall of Fame and recipient of the AIS Leadership Excellence Award.
